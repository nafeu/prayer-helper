# Prayer Breakdown

A complete guide to the number of rakats (units) for each prayer in Sunni Islam.

## Daily Prayers

| Prayer | Sunnat (Before) | Fard (Obligatory) | Sunnat (After) | Witr | Total Rakats |
|--------|----------------|-------------------|----------------|------|--------------|
| **Fajr** (Dawn) | 2 | 2 | - | - | 4 |
| **Dhuhr** (Noon) | 4 | 4 | 2 | - | 10 |
| **Asr** (Afternoon) | 4 | 4 | - | - | 8 |
| **Maghrib** (Sunset) | - | 3 | 2 | - | 5 |
| **Isha** (Night) | 4 | 4 | 2 | 3 | 13 |

## Detailed Breakdown

### Fajr (Dawn Prayer)
- **2 Rakats Sunnat (Before):** Recommended but highly emphasized
- **2 Rakats Fard (Obligatory):** Mandatory
- **Total:** 4 rakats

### Dhuhr (Noon Prayer)
- **4 Rakats Sunnat (Before):** Recommended
- **4 Rakats Fard (Obligatory):** Mandatory
- **2 Rakats Sunnat (After):** Recommended and highly emphasized
- **Total:** 10 rakats

### Asr (Afternoon Prayer)
- **4 Rakats Sunnat (Before):** Recommended
- **4 Rakats Fard (Obligatory):** Mandatory
- **Total:** 8 rakats

### Maghrib (Sunset Prayer)
- **3 Rakats Fard (Obligatory):** Mandatory
- **2 Rakats Sunnat (After):** Recommended and highly emphasized
- **Total:** 5 rakats

### Isha (Night Prayer)
- **4 Rakats Sunnat (Before):** Recommended
- **4 Rakats Fard (Obligatory):** Mandatory
- **2 Rakats Sunnat (After):** Recommended
- **3 Rakats Witr:** Recommended and highly emphasized (can be prayed as 1, 3, 5, 7, 9, or 11 rakats, but 3 is most common)
- **Total:** 13 rakats

## Notes

- **Fard:** These are the obligatory prayers that must be performed. Missing them without a valid excuse is a sin.
- **Sunnat:** These are recommended prayers following the example of the Prophet (peace be upon him). They bring additional reward but are not mandatory.
- **Witr:** This is a special prayer performed after Isha. It's highly recommended and can be prayed in odd numbers (1, 3, 5, 7, 9, or 11 rakats). The most common is 3 rakats.
- The Sunnat prayers before Fajr and after Maghrib and Dhuhr are particularly emphasized and are called "Sunnat Mu'akkadah" (confirmed Sunnah).

## Daily Total

**Total Rakats per Day:** 48 rakats
- Fard: 17 rakats
- Sunnat: 28 rakats
- Witr: 3 rakats
